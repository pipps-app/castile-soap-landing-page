Our Story

As a pharmacist, I witnessed firsthand how conventional soaps and cleaning products—filled with harsh chemicals—can irritate sensitive skin and contribute to environmental harm. This inspired me to create a better alternative: a pure, natural, and effective solution that works for the entire family.

That’s how JC Castile Liquid Soaps was born. With pharmaceutical-grade precision and a deep commitment to wellness, we craft our soaps using only plant-based ingredients. Our mission is simple: to provide safe, sustainable, and versatile products that support healthier homes, bodies, and planet.

Our Mission

To empower families, pet owners, and eco-conscious individuals with gentle, all-natural cleaning and care solutions—free from toxins, made sustainably, and designed to work for every need. We believe clean shouldn’t come at the cost of health or the environment.

Why Choose Us?

  • Versatile: one product for body care, pets, and household cleaning
